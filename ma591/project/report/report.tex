\section{Max Cut problem}

The max cut problem can be reformulate as the following form

\begin{equation}
\begin{array}{r l l l}
\max & \frac{1}{4} L \bullet X & & \\
\text{s.t.} & A_j \bullet X & \preceq & 0 \\
& j = 1,\dots, n & &
\end{array}
\end{equation}
